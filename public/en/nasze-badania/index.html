Our trials

A consumer satisfaction survey was conducted with 102 companion animals (28 cats and 74 dogs) that were supplemented with DeliGuard for period of 2 weeks. This study was to evaluate the effects of daily supplementation of DeliGuard postbiotic on health status, well-being and animal condition.

Effect of DeliGuard on the Immune System

DeliGuard supports the liver function, which results in a decrease in the CRP protein production - an indicator of inflammation in the body, which increases, for example, during illness or as a result of daily minor tissue injuries.

Improvement of liver enzymes

DeliGuard supports liver function by lowering the levels of the liver enzymes AST and ALT. Improvement of these parameters has a positive effect on the nutrition of the whole body, as well as the condition of the animal and the quality of the fur.
